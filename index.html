About Us

Carol Perkins, the dedicated attorney at the helm of the Law Office of Carol Perkins, LLC, brings a rich and diverse background to her legal practice. Initially embarking on a career in education, Carol earned a bachelor's degree in elementary education with a kindergarten endorsement, followed by a master's degree in elementary education with a gifted and talented endorsement. In her mid-30s, a determined Carol pivoted to the field of law, pursuing and successfully obtaining a Juris Doctor degree from the prestigious University of Notre Dame. Since becoming licensed in 2000, she has built a reputation as a diligent and compassionate attorney, offering expert legal services in general law, probate law, guardianships, wills, and debt collection. Carol's deep understanding of the unique dynamics of each case, coupled with a commitment to safeguarding her clients' rights, has been a cornerstone of her practice. Leveraging her educational background, she approaches each case with a nurturing perspective, ensuring personalized and empathetic legal guidance. With a history of fostering justice and navigating complex legal landscapes, Carol Perkins stands as a beacon of trust and reliability in the legal sector. Her unwavering commitment to ethical practice and legal excellence makes her a sought-after attorney, ready to guide you through your legal journey with personalized solutions and resolute advocacy.
